How We Work

Preston Wealth Advisors probe until we understand your risk tolerance’s precise latitude. Then we invest in the highest performing asset mix at a risk threshold acceptable to you. Because we manage our own portfolios, Preston Wealth Advisors has complete discretion in how and where we invest.

Protecting Your Assets

First, we make trading decisions based on data, rather than emotion. Preston Wealth Advisors has developed a proprietary, patent-pending algorithm that constantly monitors the market, identifies growth sectors, and pinpoints specific performing ETF’s and Mutual Funds. Sector agnostic, the algorithm analyzes data daily and alerts us to buy or sell based on indicators we’ve selected and continuously tune. A Preston Wealth Advisor reviews the data before ever executing a trade.

Secondly, we take more aggressive positions when the market rises and sell when indicators signal it will fall. In the event neither fixed income nor equity investments offer enough return to justify the risk, our advisors can move 100% to cash. In 2008, our algorithm protected clients, limiting their losses in our most aggressive portfolios to just 5%, when the S&P dropped 37%.

Growing Your Wealth

The same algorithm protecting clients in volatile markets maximizes their investments when it roars. Many of our retired clients avail themselves of our growth funds because they trust our algorithm to protect their principal while multiplying their assets.

Contact us now to get started growing your wealth and protecting your assets!

We do not fully adhere to a "buy and hold" strategy. We focus on market sectors, for as long as they produce. We don’t care what sector provides growth (equities, fixed income, commodities, etc.) and we hold no preconceived notions about a client’s mix of stocks, bonds, or cash at any given time. Growth is our focus. When a sector falters, we protect capital by moving it to another high performing area of the market or to cash if nothing else is working. Our portfolios average between 5-12 trading days per year.

Portfolio Management

Services

Portfolio Management

Preston Wealth Advisors offers a differentiating approach to portfolio management. Private and Institutional investors alike seem to appreciate our proprietary algorithm’s uncanny ability to predict market shifts in order to advance client objectives.

Low Duration Income Portfolio

Preston’s Low Duration Income Portfolio is a Cash/Certificate of Deposit or a Money Market alternative for clients who seek high liquidity with minimal risk, and returns greater than a savings account. This Portfolio encompasses 39 different fund choices and each sector is represented by several different funds, allowing us to assess and purchase the best performing fund in any given sector. In addition to the traditional income sectors including short-term government, municipal and corporate bonds, the Low Duration Income Portfolio also includes sectors such as global bonds, high yield funds, and total return funds to increase portfolio returns. This strategy is best suited for conservative investors.

Income Portfolio

Our Income Portfolio provides a mix of income and investment appreciation. In addition to the traditional income sectors such as short-term government, municipal and corporate bonds, the Income Portfolio also includes sectors such as global bonds, high yield funds, and convertible bond funds. By including these sectors, we provide investors with the opportunity to increase returns. The Income Portfolio encompasses 25 different fund choices and each sector is represented by several different funds, allowing Preston Wealth Advisors to assess and purchase the best performing fund in any given fund sector.

Total Return Portfolio

We designed the Total Return Portfolio for clients who want to participate in tactical asset management, but aren’t comfortable investing in aggressive growth. This portfolio can provide investors with greater stability in today’s volatile markets while providing competitive returns. In addition to the traditional income sectors such as government, municipal and corporate bonds, our Total Return Portfolio also includes sectors such as convertible securities, utility funds, and equity income funds. Including these sectors provides investors with the opportunity to participate in a total return growth strategy. The Total Return Portfolio has 40 different fund choices and each sector is represented by several different funds, thereby allowing us to assess and purchase the best performing fund in any given fund sector.

ETF Moderate Growth

Preston Wealth Advisors created the ETF Moderate Growth portfolio for capital appreciation. This portfolio functions like our ETF Growth Strategy but with a more conservative ETF universe, excluding some of the more aggressive sector funds used in the growth strategy. The Moderate Growth portfolio also has the option to “go anywhere” — 100% cash, 100% bonds or 100% equities.

ETF Growth Portfolio

Preston Wealth Advisors designed the Growth Portfolio for investors seeking maximum capital appreciation through a global tactical strategy. Unlike other funds, the Growth Portfolio has zero allocation restrictions. Based on market conditions it can go 100% cash, 100% bonds, or 100% equities. The Growth Portfolio utilizes 40 different asset class choices and each asset class encompasses several different funds. This portfolio can utilize sector specific ETF’s to help increase performance, allowing us to assess and purchase the best performing fund in any given fund sector. However, no sector can exceed 50% exposure except for cash.

Our team offers investment and financial management services to professional athletes, managing their current financial life and helping to secure their financial future, post-sports career.

You understand discipline, or you wouldn’t be a professional athlete. The same discipline that got you into the big time can turn your lucrative present into a comfortable future. Just 2 years after their football careers end, 78% of NFL players are bankrupt, or nearly there.

While career and bankruptcy statistics vary widely from sport to sport, the resounding drumbeat remains: get your financial house in order while you’re earning. Professional Athlete Services is one of our primary practices, led by managing partner and former college athlete, TJ Doremus. Our client roster includes professional athletes from the NHL and MLB. We’re knowledgeable about your unique job demands and have developed tailored concierge financial services to meet your specific needs.

First, we teach you how your finances work, so you’ll understand the decisions we make on your behalf. Then, in addition to helping you plan, save, and invest for the future, Preston Wealth Advisors helps you manage your life now. We automate your bills, provide counsel and documentation when buying a house, and even oversee everyday services such as housekeeping and pool service. Then we turn our attention to your post-sports career, helping you explore ways to capitalize on current skills and experience to launch into the next chapter of your life. Any player can live like a king for a few years. With our help, you can live like a prince for the rest of your life. Getting caught up in the spending frenzy of a professional league lifestyle is easy. Preston Wealth Advisors allows you to enjoy your earnings responsibly while protecting your net worth long term. Learn how we can help you work toward securing your financial future.
