How We Work

Preston Wealth Advisors probe until we understand your risk tolerance’s precise latitude. Then we invest in the highest performing asset mix at a risk threshold acceptable to you. Because we manage our own portfolios, Preston Wealth Advisors has complete discretion in how and where we invest.

Protecting Your Assets

First, we make trading decisions based on data, rather than emotion by using sector agnostic algorithm that analyzes data daily and alerts us to buy or sell based on indicators we have selected and continuously tune. A Preston Wealth Advisor reviews the data before ever submitting a trade.

Secondly, we take more aggressive positions when the market rises and sell when indicators signal it will fall. In the event neither fixed income nor equity investments offer enough return to justify the risk, the portfolios can move 100% to cash. In 2008, the algorithm would have limited the losses in our most aggressive portfolios to just 5%, when the S&P dropped 37%.

PWA Tactical Portfolios

PWA Tactical Portfolios are relative strength, momentum based strategies that monitor price movement and statistical growth opportunities among a range of investments selected from the portfolio’s investment universe.  Using a mathematical algorithm and an exclusive scoring methodology, a combination of investments are selected based off of the opportunity present in the market and the overall portfolio investment objective.  A wide spectrum of portfolios are offered to cater to varying risk tolerances and investment objectives.

PWA Tactical Portfolios

PWA ETF Conservative Growth Portfolio

PWA’s ETF Conservative Growth Portfolio is designed for investors seeking capital appreciation with a lower risk tolerance. The ETF Conservative Growth strategy will invest in broad equity sector funds with no sector specific ETF exposure. This portfolio utilizes 8 positions to further diversify risk. A full complement of fixed-income ETF’s are also available for the model to choose from. Investments will be allocated between equities and fixed-income based upon an analyses of market conditions. No single position will account for more than 50% of the portfolio and if our indicators signal a downtrend in either the equity or fixed-income space, the portfolio can be moved partially or entirely to cash in order to seek preservation of your principal investment. The strategy seeks to outperform a 30% Standard & Poor’s 500 Total Return Index (“S&P 500®”) and 70% Barclay’s Aggregate Bond Index (“Barclays”) blend. Investments involve risk and you could lose the principal invested.

PWA Total Return Portfolio

We designed the Total Return Portfolio for clients who are seeking growth primarily through capital appreciation and secondarily through income. This portfolio can provide investors with greater stability in today’s volatile markets while providing competitive returns. In addition to the traditional income sectors such as government, municipal and corporate bonds, our Total Return Portfolio also includes sectors such as convertible securities, utility funds, and equity income funds. Including these sectors provides investors with the opportunity to participate in a total return growth strategy. The Total Return Portfolio has 40 different fund choices and each sector is represented by several different funds, thereby allowing us to try and assess and purchase the best performing fund in any given fund sector.

PWA ETF Moderate Growth Portfolio

We created the ETF Moderate Growth portfolio for capital appreciation with a moderate risk tolerance. This portfolio functions like our ETF Growth Strategy but with a more conservative ETF universe, excluding some of the more aggressive sector funds used in the growth strategy, however, this portfolio may consist of either domestic, international sector-specific ETFs or commodity ETFs. The Moderate Growth portfolio does not necessarily have bond exposure at any given time, but retains the ability to utilize bonds or cash in order to attempt to protect principals.

PWA ETF Growth Portfolio

We developed the Growth Portfolio for investors seeking maximum capital appreciation through a global tactical strategy. Unlike other funds, the Growth Portfolio has zero allocation restrictions and based on market conditions can go 100% cash, 100% bonds, or 100% equities. The Growth Portfolio utilizes 40 different asset class choices and each asset class encompasses several different funds. This portfolio can utilize sector specific ETF’s to help increase performance, allowing us to seek to assess and purchase the best performing fund in any given fund sector. However, no sector can exceed 50% exposure except for cash.

PWA OTC Hedge Portfolio

PWA’s OTC Hedge portfolio is designed for investors who are seeking to enhance investment performance while reducing risk and attempting to protect their assets from marketing volatility and uncertainty. The PWA OTC Hedge holds 1 position at a time going either long or short the Nasdaq 100 (via and ETF) or investing in long-term Treasury bonds or cash.

PWA Low Duration Portfolio

Our Low Duration Income Portfolio is a Cash/Certificate of Deposit or a Money Market alternative for clients who seek high liquidity with minimal risk, and returns greater than a savings account. This Portfolio encompasses 39 different fund choices and each sector is represented by several different funds, allowing us to assess and purchase what we believe to be the best performing fund in any given sector. In addition to the traditional income sectors including short-term government, municipal and corporate bonds, the Low Duration Income Portfolio also includes sectors such as global bonds, high yield funds, and total return funds to help increase portfolio returns. This strategy is best suited for conservative investors and is currently closed to new investors.

PWA Dynamic Portfolios

PWA Dynamic Portfolios use a quantitatively based, absolute return strategy with exposure to the U.S. Treasury market.  Aside from the PWA Dynamic Treasury Portfolio, the PWA Dynamic Portfolios utilizes multiple uncorrelated tactical strategies to capture price trends and positions assets to benefit from a rising or declining yield curve.  These blended Portfolios provide access to a hybrid approach blending the relative strength based PWA Tactical portfolios and the absolute return strategy.  The segment of the portfolio dedicated to U.S. Treasury exposure is designed to enhance active premium and dynamic risk protection.  Should you have interest in solely using a U.S. Treasury based portfolio as a standalone investment, PWA is happy to provide the solution by offering the PWA Dynamic Treasury portfolio.

PWA Dynamic Portfolios

PWA Dynamic Treasury Portfolio

Our PWA Dynamic Treasury Portfolio is intended for investors who are seeking a more aggressive way to invest in the U.S. Treasury markets or utilize a strategy to provide non-correlation to their outside equity positions. This portfolio aims to take advantage of volatility in the US 20-30 Year Treasury by actively trading several mutual funds within the strategy. The portfolio will consist of 1 mutual fund at a time. Like all PWA portfolios, cash is always an option for our portfolio managers. The portfolio managers will either go long or short based on the current market conditions of the U.S. 20-30 Year Term Treasury.

PWA Dynamic Income Portfolio

We conceived the Dynamic Income Portfolio for investors who are seeking a mix of income, investment appreciation and to take advantage of market volatility in the U.S. Treasury markets. In addition to traditional sectors such as short-term government, municipal and corporate bonds, this portfolio can also include sectors such as global bonds, high yield bonds, convertible bonds, dividend paying companies and utilities to name a few. As a general rule, no single sector other than cash will make up more than 50% of the portfolio at any given time. 2 “static” positions can be used to provide additional yield, as well as to provide stability through non-correlation to the equity markets. Within the portfolio, our portfolio managers will utilize a long/short U.S. Treasury (PWA Dynamic Treasury) strategy in order to try and take advantage of the volatility in the Treasury markets. 3 scenarios are possible on this side of the portfolio: Long, Short or Cash.

PWA Dynamic Growth Portfolio

Our PWA Dynamic Growth Portfolio is designed for investors who are seeking capital appreciation with a hedging feature providing non-correlation to the equity markets. 60% of the portfolio will be invested in the PWA ETF Growth portfolio and 40% of the portfolio will be invested in the PWA Dynamic Treasury portfolio. In addition to traditional equity sectors, the PWA ETF Growth portfolio includes a full complement of fixed-income sectors such as government bonds, convertible funds, high yield bonds and inverse funds. PWA utilizes both domestic and international sector specific funds, as well as a full range of commodity funds in order to seek the best possible return. Outperforming sectors will be over-weighted in order to maximize returns. No single position will account for more than 50% of the portfolio and if our indicators signal a downtrend in either the equity or fixed-income space, the portfolio can be moved partially or entirely to cash in order to seek preservation of the principal investment. To provide non-correlation, our portfolio managers will utilize a long/short US Treasury (PWA Dynamic Treasury) strategy in order to try and take advantage of the volatility in the Treasury markets. 3 scenarios are possible on this side of the portfolio: Long, Short or Cash.

PWA Dynamic Hedge Portfolio

PWA’s Dynamic Hedge Portfolio is formulated for investors who are seeking to enhance investment performance while reducing risk and protecting their assets from typical market volatility and uncertainty. The portfolio is made up of 2 separate strategies running simultaneously. On the equity side, only 1 position is held at a time, investing in either the OTC 100, the OTC Short 100, long-term Treasury bonds or cash. Utilizing a disciplined tactical approach in the Dynamic Hedge Portfolio, our advisors buy and sell 1 of these 3 carefully selected ETFs based on current market conditions. If our indicators signal a downtrend, the entire portfolio can be moved to cash in an attempt to preserve the principal investment. On the fixed income side, our portfolio managers will utilize a long/short US Treasury (PWA Dynamic Treasury) strategy in order to try and take advantage of the volatility in Treasury markets. There are 3 possible scenarios on this side of the portfolio: Long, Short or Cash.

Why We Collect and How We Use Information:

We limit the collection and use of Information within our firm to only those individuals associated or employed with us that must have Information to provide financial services to you. Such services include but are not limited to maintaining your accounts, processing transaction requests, and providing the advisory services described in our Form ADV and our ADV Brochure.

How We Gather Information:

Most of the Information we gather comes directly from you when you provide us with information on or from any of the following sources:

  • Applications or forms (i.e. name, address, social security number, birth date, assets, income, financial history)
  • Transactional activity in your account (i.e. trading history, account balances)
  • Information services and consumer reporting sources (i.e. to verify your identity or to assess your credit history)
  • Other sources with your consent (i.e. your insurance professional, attorney or accountant)

How We Protect Information:

Our employees and affiliated persons are required to protect the confidentiality of Information and to comply with our stated policies. They may access Information only when there is an acceptable reason to do so, such as to service your account or provide you with financial services. Employees who violate our Privacy Policy are subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination from employment with us. We also maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect Information, which comply with applicable SEC, state, and federal laws.

Sharing Information with Other Companies Permitted Under Law:

We do not disclose Information obtained in the course of our practice except as required or permitted under law. Permitted disclosures include, for instance, providing Information to unrelated third parties who need to know such Information in order to assist us with providing services to you. Unrelated third parties may include broker/dealers, mutual fund companies, insurance companies, and the custodian with which your assets are held. In such situations, we stress the confidential nature of Information being shared.
